xml_store orbeon stuff

Orbeon is invoked by delivering the form through a jsp situated in the directory.

      /<PATH TO>/tomcat/webapps/orbeon/xforms-jsp/mei-form

Orbeon interpretes everything deliverd that way as being xforms. The mei_form.jsp is a kind of proxy executing xinclude.

You should also

      cp properties-local.xml /<PATH TO>/tomcat/webapps/orbeon/WEB-INF/resources/config/properties-local.xml
